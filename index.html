About Pfizer

Pfizer is a global pharmaceutical corporation headquartered in New York. With over 170 years of innovation, Pfizer is committed to developing vaccines and therapies that help improve global health. The company is one of the world's largest and most respected pharmaceutical brands, known for its dedication to research and breakthroughs in medicine. Pfizer played a key role in responding to global health crises, including the COVID-19 pandemic, by developing effective vaccines. The company actively invests in cutting-edge technology and maintains strict quality standards to ensure the safety and effectiveness of its medications. Pfizer continues to collaborate with healthcare providers, governments, and communities worldwide to make healthcare more accessible and equitable.
